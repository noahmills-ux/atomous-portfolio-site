About Atomous

Atomous is a professional marching percussionist and soloist that currently resides in the United States. He began his musical career at the young age of 10 performing with the Colonial Williamsburg Fifes and Drums. In March 2016, at the age of 15, Atomous started his own YouTube channel. Since creating his channel, he has gone on to produce many entertaining drum related videos for his growing fanbase. As of today, Atomous has over 5 millions views on his videos and over 20,000 subscribers. Atomous continues to this day making content, collaborating with other content creators, and writing music for his growing fanbase.
